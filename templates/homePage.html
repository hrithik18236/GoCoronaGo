About Our Aim    

When almost the entire world is under lockdown, people are resolving to a myriad of measures to tackle several issues. In lieu of such events, organisations, as well as individuals from multiple sectors, are suffering an economic backlash. We at IvyOpus aim to ease and eradicate a lot of tension for such individuals by providing a platform for laid-off employees as well as individuals from the unorganised or organised sector to have the opportunity to be re-employed. Badges would be deployed by the group's sponsors to verify their financial commitments, make the restrictions on previous financial obligations more flexible and provide emergency operating contracts to users.
